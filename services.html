Our Services

Professional furnace and air conditioning maintenance in Renfrew County. Reliable solutions to keep your home comfortable year-round.

Furnace Maintenance

Our technicians perform a comprehensive 20-point inspection and cleaning to ensure your heating system remains safe and efficient through the winter.

Service Checklist

1
Heat Exchanger & Burners — Detailed inspection for cracks, wear, or soot buildup.
2
Blower & Filter — Cleaning of the blower assembly and professional filter replacement.
3
Safety & Controls — Testing of all limit switches and carbon monoxide monitoring.

Why Schedule Annual Service?

  • Lower energy consumption
  • Validates manufacturer warranties
  • Identifies potential issues before failure

Cooling & Heat Pumps

Precision tuning for air conditioning units and heat pumps to maintain peak cooling performance and indoor air quality.

Service Checklist

1
Coil Sanitization — Cleaning of evaporator and condenser coils for airflow.
2
Electrical & Refrigerant — Checking line pressures and tightening all connections.
3
Condensate Systems — Clearing drain lines to prevent blockages or leaks.

Benefits of AC Tuning

  • Consistent humidity control
  • Extended equipment lifespan
  • Optimized seasonal electricity costs
