Furnace Maintenance

We perform a thorough 20+ point inspection and cleaning to keep your furnace safe, efficient, and reliable through the harsh Renfrew County winters.

Step-by-Step Furnace Maintenance Process

1
Inspect & Clean — Check heat exchanger, burners, and flue for cracks or soot buildup.
2
Filter Service — Replace or clean air filter and inspect blower assembly.
3
Safety Check — Test all safety controls, limit switches, and carbon monoxide levels.
4
Lubricate & Test — Lubricate motors, check electrical connections, and test thermostat response.
5
Final Test Run — Run the system through a full heating cycle and verify proper operation.

Why annual furnace maintenance matters in Renfrew County:

  • • Prevents dangerous carbon monoxide leaks
  • • Improves energy efficiency and lowers heating bills
  • • Extends the life of your furnace
  • • Maintains manufacturer warranty

Air Conditioning & Heat Pump Maintenance

Our comprehensive AC tune-up keeps your cooling system running at peak performance and helps prevent breakdowns during hot summer months.

Step-by-Step AC Maintenance Process

1
System Inspection — Check refrigerant levels, electrical connections, and overall condition.
2
Clean Coils — Thoroughly clean condenser and evaporator coils for maximum efficiency.
3
Drain Line Service — Clear condensate drain lines and pan to prevent water damage.
4
Filter & Blower — Replace or clean air filter and inspect blower motor and wheel.
5
Performance Test — Test temperature differential, pressures, and overall system operation.

Benefits of annual AC maintenance:

  • • Lower energy bills and improved cooling performance
  • • Longer equipment lifespan
  • • Fewer emergency repairs during heat waves
  • • Better indoor air quality
